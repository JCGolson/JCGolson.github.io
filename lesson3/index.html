Gem State News

gemstatenews.com

Purpose

  • The Gem State News home page will provide visitors with current weather stories with images on the home page. It will have individual pages for the three towns of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, which will have current weather conditions along with pertinent information about the town and relevant articles pertaining to that town.
  • By providing the current weather conditions for these towns, residents can determine whether they can do the desired outdoor activities they are planning. They will also be able to find out about upcoming events in their communities.
  • The storm center will contain a form that will collect data from users who want to report on their weather.
