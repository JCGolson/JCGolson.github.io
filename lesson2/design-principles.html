White Space and Clean Design

The Family History Guide

thefhguide.com/

This site has lots of white space along the sides that helps it to look very clean and easy to navigate. There is white space between items, too, to keep it from looking crowded.

Proximitiy

This Is Why I'm Full

thisiswhyimfull.com/recipe-index/

With this site, the proximity of the recipe title, photo, recipe info and the Read More button shows that they are related to each other.

Visual Hierarchy

Easy Order Banners

easyorderbanners.com/

This site use Visual Hierarchy well with the large title over the image of the banner and then the Shop Now button and the smaller detail just above that. Farther down the page, the Visual Hierarchy is using font sizes and images above the text to draw the eye to them.
